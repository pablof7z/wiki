Wikipedia has been captured

"There is no real guarantee that Wikipedia is not entirely controlled by the CIA."
— Larry Sanger, Wikipedia co-founder, on Tucker Carlson
🕵️
Shadow Admins
Anonymous editors with hidden identities make irreversible decisions. Who are they? What are their biases? Nobody knows.
🚫
Blacklisted Sources
Conservative outlets, alternative media, and dissenting voices are systematically excluded. One perspective enforced as "neutral."
🔒
Forced Consensus
Complex topics reduced to a single "approved" narrative. Nuance is eliminated. Disagreement is deleted.
🎭
Fake Neutrality
"Neutral Point of View" is a myth. Every edit, every source selection, every word choice reflects bias. Wikipedia just hides it.

Two core principles

1.
There is a reality, but we can't fully grasp it.
Truth exists, but our perception is always filtered through experience, culture, and belief. Instead of pretending we can achieve perfect objectivity, we should embrace our limitations.
2.
In knowledge, consensus is tyranny.
Forcing agreement on complex topics destroys nuance. The richness of human understanding comes from integrating multiple perspectives, not reducing them to a single approved narrative.

Wikipedia is broken. Wikifreedia is the fix.
