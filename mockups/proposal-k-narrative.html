Ivermectin
Ivermectin is an antiparasitic drug used to treat several neglected tropical diseases.[1] During the COVID-19 pandemic, misinformation about ivermectin as a treatment spread widely on social media.[2] Health authorities including the FDA, EMA, and WHO advised against its use for COVID-19 outside of clinical trials, as systematic reviews found insufficient evidence of benefit.[3][4]
