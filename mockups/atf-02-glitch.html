Ivermectin
A Nobel Prize-winning drug that doctors around the world were using to treat COVID with real results — until the establishment panicked. They called it "horse paste" to protect a hundred-billion-dollar vaccine rollout. Follow the money.
An antiparasitic with no demonstrated benefit for COVID-19 in randomized controlled trials. In vitro activity does not equal clinical efficacy. The gold-standard evidence is clear: the trials do not show benefit. Anecdotes are not data.
A cheap generic drug the regulatory system was structurally incapable of honestly evaluating. The EUA required no adequate alternative to exist. The vaccine program depended on the answer being no. You don't need a conspiracy — just an incentive structure.
